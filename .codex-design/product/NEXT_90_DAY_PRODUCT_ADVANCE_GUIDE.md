# Chummer next 90-day product advance

## Framing

The flagship closeout frontier is the last forced concentration pass that turns Chummer into a believable replacement product.

The next quarter should not reopen architecture cleanup, repo splitting, or horizon sprawl.
It should take the now-hard flagship bar and make it boring:

1. repeatable desktop release truth,
2. oracle-backed veteran replacement proof,
3. desktop-native trust and recovery,
4. conflict-safe continuity across claimed devices,
5. first-party artifact factory and public proof shelf truth,
6. campaign cold opens, mission briefings, build explain companions, and runsite host bundles,
7. deeper campaign operations and GM/organizer prep,
8. stronger Build / Rules / Exchange moat,
9. creator publication, artifact shelves, organizer/community operations, guided onboarding, public concierge and trust surfaces, and launch health,
10. measured product-governor cadence.

The machine-readable control plane for this follow-on wave lives in `NEXT_90_DAY_PRODUCT_ADVANCE_REGISTRY.yaml`.

## Activation rule

This wave is seeded now so Fleet and repo owners can stage it immediately.

It activates when:

1. the current full-product flagship closeout frontier reaches zero open milestones,
2. `FLAGSHIP_PRODUCT_READINESS.generated.json` is green on `desktop_client` and the other flagship lanes,
3. the promoted desktop route is honest on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Until then, this wave should shape follow-on work without preempting the current flagship closeout. Spare Fleet shards may already execute successor-wave slices, but they must not steal shards that are still assigned to the current closeout frontier.

## Ordering rule

1. Productionize native-host desktop release and promotion first.
2. Turn Chummer5a parity into oracle-backed release truth second.
3. Make desktop-native trust, recovery, and workspace continuity boring third.
4. Turn release, support, build, campaign, and runsite truth into first-party artifact bundles fourth.
5. Pull the preserved Next Big Wins forward into a broader successor tranche for campaign operations, rules/build/exchange moat, creator/community publication, onboarding, and public launch health.
6. Keep the product-governor loop measured and weekly instead of heroic and ad hoc.
