# Glossary

## Artifact

A publishable or previewable output with manifest, provenance, and lifecycle state.

## Artifact studio

The short-to-medium-form output family behind JACKPOINT: dossiers, recaps, briefings, evidence rooms, and share-safe previews.

## Booster

The supporter-facing name for the bounded premium participation lane.

## Booster-first

An access posture for unusually expensive early lanes. It is not a permanent paywall promise.

## Coach

A governed, grounded assistance surface for players or operators. It may help explain or guide, but it does not become rules truth or session truth.

## Director

A higher-order hosted orchestration role that coordinates bounded multi-step flows above individual Coach or Spider interactions.

## Horizon

A future-capability lane with canon and an eventual build path, but not yet a shipment promise.

## Jury

The final landing and review authority. Booster lanes, helpers, and automation do not bypass it.

## Knowledge fabric

The future lane for grounded, cited rules understanding built from Chummer-owned truth rather than assistant folklore.

## Participant lane

Fleet's operator term for the temporary execution lane behind a booster or participation session.

## Proof shelf

The public place where Chummer shows what is real now, with honest labels for available, preview, and horizon surfaces.

## Provenance

The chain showing where an output came from, which canon it used, and how it was produced.

## Public surface

A downstream user-visible route or build such as `chummer.run`, `/downloads`, or the public guide.

## Receipt

The evidence record attached to a mechanics result, render job, sponsor contribution, or release action.

## Runsite

A bounded explorable location pack for mission-space artifacts. It is an artifact lane, not live session truth.

## Sponsor session

The Hub-owned participation record for a bounded premium or booster help session.

## Spider

A governed hosted operator/support role around session relay, coordination, or intervention. It does not own mechanics or registry truth.
